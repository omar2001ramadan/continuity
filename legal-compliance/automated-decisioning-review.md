# Automated Decisioning Review

Status: mainnet-gated legal checklist.

Scoring providers must document domains, thresholds, appeal paths, human review requirements, and automated decisioning limitations before production use.

Required evidence:
- Owner: Legal/compliance lead.
- Status: required before default scorer promotion.
- Evidence links: model card, evaluation report, human-review rules, appeal policy, threshold cost model, and automated-decisioning memo.
- Blocking criteria: missing human review for high-impact labels or missing model limitations blocks promotion.
