# Appeal and Takedown Policy

Status: mainnet-gated legal checklist.

High-impact labels, negative attestations, and public warnings require evidence commitments, appeal channels, reversal state, and jurisdiction-specific review.

Required evidence:
- Owner: Legal/compliance lead.
- Status: required before public high-impact labels.
- Evidence links: appeal intake workflow, reviewer SLA, reversal object examples, claimant notice template, and jurisdiction review memo.
- Blocking criteria: no appeal path, no reversal state, or unsupported public negative claim blocks promotion.
