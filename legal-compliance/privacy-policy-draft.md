# Privacy Policy Draft

Status: mainnet-gated legal checklist.

Production use requires jurisdiction-specific legal review before collecting or processing private evidence, metadata, or risk assessment data.

Required evidence:
- Owner: Legal/privacy lead.
- Status: required before hosted production data collection.
- Evidence links: data inventory, consent language, retention schedule, deletion/export workflow, processor list, and jurisdiction review memo.
- Blocking criteria: processing private evidence without consent/retention mapping blocks promotion.
