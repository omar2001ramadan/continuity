# Abuse Campaign Runbook

Owner: Abuse response lead.
Status: required before `TSL-MAINNET`.

Trigger: coordinated spam, Sybil farming, malicious negative attestations, or provider abuse is detected.

Steps:
- Classify attack type and affected domains.
- Preserve evidence commitments without exposing private content.
- Rate-limit or quarantine abusive issuers/providers/relays according to governance policy.
- Ensure negative claims have appeal and reversal paths.
- Add or update deterministic abuse regression vectors.

Evidence: abuse case ID, evidence commitments, policy action, appeal handling record, and regression vector.
