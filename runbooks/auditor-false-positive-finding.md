# Auditor False Positive Finding Runbook

Status: draft
Owner: protocol-governance

Detection signals: relay dispute, independent auditor disagreement, replay success, or malformed finding evidence.

Immediate response: freeze automatic escalation, replay the challenged segment, request auditor evidence, publish corrected status, and preserve both signed claims.

Recovery evidence required for approval: replay transcript, finding hash, correction notice, auditor response, and governance review.
